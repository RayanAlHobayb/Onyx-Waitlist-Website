FAQ

Common
questions

Is Onyx Sharia-compliant? +

Yes. Onyx is built from the ground up to be fully Sharia-compliant. There is no interest (riba). The card operates on a Murabaha structure reviewed by our Sharia advisory board.

Do I need to change how I pay rent? +

No. Keep paying rent however you currently do — through Ejar, bank transfer, or directly to your landlord. Onyx verifies your payment and credits the points separately. Nothing changes on your landlord's end.

What is the minimum spend? +

Spend SAR 1,000 per month on everyday purchases with your Onyx card to activate rent rewards. That's about SAR 33 a day — groceries, dining, coffee, fuel. Most cardholders hit this without thinking about it.

Is the card free? +

Yes. The standard Onyx card is completely free. A premium tier (SAR 350/year) is available with higher earn rates on rent and partner spending, for those who want to maximize their rewards.

When does Onyx launch? +

We're launching in Riyadh first. Join the waitlist and we'll notify you as soon as we're ready. Early waitlist members get priority access.
