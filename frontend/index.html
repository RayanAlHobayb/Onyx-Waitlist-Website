FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to change how I pay rent?

No. Keep paying however you do now. Onyx verifies through Ejar and credits points separately. Nothing changes on your landlord's end.

What is the minimum spend?

SAR 1,000/month on everyday purchases. That's about SAR 33 a day. Most people hit this naturally.

Is the card free?

Yes. Free to get, free to use. Start earning from your first transaction.

How do I earn on rent if I don't pay rent with a card?

You don't pay rent through the card. You pay rent via bank transfer as usual. Onyx verifies the payment through Ejar contract data and credits points to your card account separately.
